Maysoon Zayid Biography

Maysoon Zayid is an American actress and comedian. She is well recognized for being one of America’s first Muslim women comedians. She was also the first person to ever perform stand-up in Palestine and Jordan.

Zayid contributes three months a year in the Palestinian territories, managing an arts program for disabled and orphaned children in refugee camps. She assists the children to use art to deal with trauma and bridge the gap between disabled and non-disabled children. Eighty percent of the funding for the camps comes from her comedy work.

    Maysoon Zayid Comedian

    In 2003, Zayid paired with fellow comedian and Paramus native Dean Obeidallah to launch the New York Arab-American Comedy Festival. During her early acting years, she found her disability and ethnicity repeatedly limiting her advancement.

    Zayid later moved on to stand-up. She started being featured at New York’s top clubs, including Caroline’s, Gotham, and Stand Up NY, where she takes on serious topics such as terrorism and the Israel-Palestine conflict.

    In late 2006, Zayid started off her one-woman show Little American Whore (LAW) at Los Angeles’s Comedy Central stage. The show was produced and directed by Kathy Najimy. In 2008, LAW’s screenplay was chosen for the Sundance Screenwriters Lab. Production started with Zayid as the lead in the fall of 2009.

    She later has a role in the 2008 Adam Sandler film, You Don’t Mess with the Zohan. Zayid regularly tours alone or as a special guest on the Axis of Evil Comedy Tour. She also co-hosts the radio show Fann Majnoon with Obeidallah.

    Maysoon Zayid Disability | Palsy

    She was born with cerebral palsy. Zayid described herself in a BBC interview as “a Palestinian Muslim virgin with cerebral palsy, from New Jersey, who is an actress, comedian, and activist”. She walked in New York Fashion Week and is proud to be an ambassador for the Cerebral Palsy Foundation.

    Maysoon Zayid General Hospital

    Zayid had frequently dreamed of appearing on the ABC daytime soap opera General Hospital. In June 2019 she debuted in the recurring role of Zahra Amir in the series.

    Maysoon Zayid Book

    Find Another Dream, written and performed by Zayid, is the true story of how a Muslim Jersey girl with cerebral palsy created her own path to stardom.
