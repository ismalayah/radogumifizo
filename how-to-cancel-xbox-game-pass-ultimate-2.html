September 2024 · 6 minute read

How do I cancel my ultimate game pass?

You can cancel your Game Pass Ultimate subscription at any time by signing in to your account. Under Xbox Game Pass Ultimate, select Payment & billing, then Cancel. You’ll have an option to end your subscription on its expiration date.

Why can’t I cancel my Xbox game pass Ultimate?

The first is that users cannot cancel their Xbox Game Pass if it is suspended or has a past-due balance; the account must be paid and in good standing to be canceled. The second is that prepaid subscriptions cannot be canceled; they expire automatically at the end of the subscription period.

Can I cancel Xbox game pass anytime?

You can cancel your Xbox Game Pass Ultimate subscription at any time, and you’ll continue to have access to your Ultimate benefits until your prepaid time ends, at which point you won’t be charged again. You can also cancel your subscription from your console.

How do I turn off autopay on Xbox game pass?

On the Account screen, select “Subscriptions.” Once you open Subscriptions preferences on your Xbox, select “Game Pass.” Finally, select “Turn Off Recurring Billing” to stop paying for Game Pass every month.

How do I unsubscribe from EA Play?

Canceling your EA Play membership is quick and easy. From the nav bar select EA Play > Manage My Membership. Just click the “Cancel my membership” link at the bottom to get started. No fuss, no muss.

Will I lose my games if I cancel game pass?

No, if you cancel your Xbox Game Pass subscription, you’ll lose access to any games you downloaded while you were subscribed. Games are available to play, in full, only if you continue to pay for the service. If you want to continue playing a game, you’ll need to buy it separately!

What happens if a game is removed from Game Pass?

What happens if a game is removed from GamePass? When a game leaves the Xbox Game Pass library, you won’t be able to play it any longer, even if it’s downloaded to your console. With both the games-on-demand services, once games are gone, they’re gone.

What happens if you stop paying for Xbox Game Pass?

You will no longer be able to play online multiplayer, and you will lose access to Gold-exclusive deals and Games With Gold. These won’t come back until you purchase another subscription.
